German words: (English translation, English pronunciation)

* Was ist das? (What is that?, Vaas ist daas?)
* Wie heißt das auf Deutsch? (What is that called in German?, Vee haysst daas owf Doytch?)
* Was bedeutet das? (What does that mean?, Vaas be-doy-tet daas?)

Tips:

* If you are pointing at something, you can say "Was ist das?" or "Wie heißt das auf Deutsch?"
* If you are hearing a word or phrase and don't know what it means, you can say "Was bedeutet das?"
* You can also use a dictionary or translation app to help you learn new words and phrases.

Specifically for asking what something is, I recommend using "Was ist das?" or "Wie heißt das auf Deutsch?" These phrases are both polite and direct, and they are likely to be understood by native German speakers.
