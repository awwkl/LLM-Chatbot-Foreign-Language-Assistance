German words: (English translation, English pronunciation!!!):

* Wie kann ich den Preis verhandeln oder handeln? (How can I negotiate or bargain the price? / vee kahn ihk dehn prais fer-hond-eln oh-der han-deln?)
* Kann ich einen Rabatt bekommen? (Can I get a discount? / kann ihk ai-nen ra-bat be-kom-men?)
* Ist der Preis verhandelbar? (Is the price negotiable? / ist der prais fer-han-del-bar?)
* Ich würde gerne den Preis verhandeln. (I would like to negotiate the price. / ih vürd-e ger-ne dehn prais fer-han-del-n.)
* Können wir uns auf einen Preis einigen? (Can we agree on a price? / kön-nen vir uns auf ai-nen prais ai-ni-gen?)

Examples of how to use these phrases:

* "Wie kann ich den Preis verhandeln?" (How can I negotiate the price?)
* "Kann ich einen Rabatt bekommen für den Mantel?" (Can I get a discount on the coat?)
* "Ist der Preis des Autos verhandelbar?" (Is the price of the car negotiable?)
* "Ich würde gerne den Preis des Fernsehers verhandeln." (I would like to negotiate the price of the television.)
* "Können wir uns auf einen Preis von 50 Euro einigen?" (Can we agree on a price of 50 euros?)

Tips for negotiating or bargaining in German:

* Be polite and respectful, even if you are trying to get a lower price.
* Be realistic in your expectations. You are unlikely to get a 50% discount on a new car.
* Be prepared to walk away if you are not happy with the price.
* Be patient. It may take some time to negotiate a price that you are both happy with.

Here are some additional tips that are specific to negotiating or bargaining in German:

* Use the phrase "Ich würde gerne den Preis verhandeln" (I would like to negotiate the price) to let the seller know that you are interested in getting a lower price.
* Be specific about how much you are willing to pay. For example, instead of saying "Kann ich einen Rabatt bekommen?" (Can I get a discount?), you could say "Kann ich einen Rabatt von 10% bekommen?" (Can I get a 10% discount?)
* Be prepared to compromise. It is unlikely that you will get everything you want, but you should be able to negotiate a price that is fair to both you and the seller.
* Don't be afraid to walk away. If the seller is not willing to negotiate the price, you can always walk away and find another seller.
