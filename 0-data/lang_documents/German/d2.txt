German words: (English translation, pronunciation)

* Wo ist das nächste XXX? (Where is the nearest XXX?, Vo ist das nexte XXX?)
* Entschuldigung, wo ist das nächste XXX? (Excuse me, where is the nearest XXX?, Entshuldigung, vo ist das nexte XXX?)
* Können Sie mir bitte sagen, wo das nächste XXX ist? (Can you please tell me where the nearest XXX is?, Kennen zi mir bitte zagen, vo das nexte XXX ist?)

Tips:

* Use the word "XXX" in place of the specific name of the place you are looking for, such as "Restaurant", "Toilette" (toilet), or "Geldautomat" (ATM).
* If you are not sure how to pronounce a word, you can ask the person you are speaking to for help.
* Be polite and respectful, especially if you are asking for help from a stranger.

Here are some examples of how to use these phrases in a conversation:

* Excuse me, where is the nearest toilet? - Entschuldigung, wo ist die nächste Toilette? (Entshuldigung, vo ist di nexte Toy-let-uh?)
* Can you please tell me where the nearest ATM is? - Können Sie mir bitte sagen, wo der nächste Geldautomat ist? (Kennen zi mir bitte zagen, vo der nexte Geld-aut-oh-mat ist?)
* Where is the nearest restaurant? - Wo ist das nächste Restaurant? (Vo ist das nexte Res-tau-rahnt?)

If you are new to the German language, I recommend that you start by learning some basic phrases and vocabulary. There are many resources available online and in libraries to help you learn German. Once you have a basic understanding of the language, you can start to practice speaking and listening to German with native speakers.
