Here are some basic Spanish phrases for beginners:

* Spanish words: (English translation, English pronunciation)
    * Hola: Hello (OH-lah)
    * Adiós: Goodbye (ah-dee-OHS)
    * Por favor: Please (por fah-BOHR)
    * Gracias: Thank you (grah-SEE-ahs)
    * De nada: You're welcome (deh NAH-dah)
    * Disculpe: Excuse me (dees-KOOL-peh)
    * Lo siento: I'm sorry (loh see-EN-toh)
    * ¿Cómo estás?: How are you? (KOH-moh es-TAHS?)
    * Bien, ¿y tú?: I'm good, and you? (bee-EN, ¿ee TOO?)
    * ¿Hablas inglés?: Do you speak English? (¿AH-blahs een-GLEHS?)
    * Sí: Yes (see)
    * No: No (noh)
    * ¿Cuánto cuesta?: How much is this? (¿KWAHN-toh KWEH-stah?)
    * ¿Dónde está el baño?: Where is the bathroom? (¿DOHN-deh es-TAH el BAHN-yo?)
    * ¿Qué hora es?: What time is it? (¿keh OH-rah es?)

Tips for learning basic Spanish phrases:

* Start by learning the most common phrases, such as greetings, polite expressions, and questions about basic needs.
* Practice the phrases aloud until you can say them without hesitation.
* Don't be afraid to make mistakes. Everyone makes mistakes when they are learning a new language.
* Try to use the phrases in real-world conversations, even if you are just talking to a friend or family member.
* There are many resources available to help you learn Spanish, such as books, apps, and online courses. Find a resource that works for you and stick with it.
