To ask if you can pay in cash or card in Mandarin, you can use the following phrases:

* 可以现金支付吗？ (kěyǐ xiànjīn zhīfù ma?) - Can I pay in cash?
* 可以刷卡吗？ (kěyǐ shuākǎ ma?) - Can I pay by card?

You can also use the following phrase:

* 可以现金或刷卡吗？ (kěyǐ xiànjīn huò shuākǎ ma?) - Can I pay in cash or by card?

Pronunciation:

* 可以现金支付吗？ (kěyǐ xiànjīn zhīfù ma?) - kè-yǐ shén-jīn zhī-fù-ma?
* 可以刷卡吗？ (kěyǐ shuākǎ ma?) - kè-yǐ shuā-kǎ ma?
* 可以现金或刷卡吗？ (kěyǐ xiànjīn huò shuākǎ ma?) - kè-yǐ shén-jīn hò shuā-kǎ ma?

Tips:

* The word for "cash" is 现金 (xiànjīn).
* The word for "card" is 卡 (kǎ).
* The verb "to pay" is 支付 (zhīfù).
* The phrase "or" is 或 (huò).
* When you're asking a question in Mandarin, you usually put the question word at the beginning of the sentence.
* You can also use the phrase 可以吗？ (kěyǐ ma?) at the end of your sentence to make it more polite.

Example:

* 你好，请问可以现金支付吗？ (nǐ hǎo, qǐngwèn kěyǐ xiànjīn zhīfù ma?) - Hello, can I pay in cash?
