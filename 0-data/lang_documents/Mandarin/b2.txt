Here are some basic Mandarin phrases for someone new to the language, formatted in point format, with pronunciation:

* 你好 (nǐ hǎo) - Hello
* 谢谢 (xiè xiè) - Thank you
* 不客气 (bú kè qi) - You're welcome
* 对不起 (duì bù qǐ) - Sorry
* 再见 (zài jiàn) - Goodbye
* 是 (shì) - Yes
* 不是 (bú shì) - No
* 我不知道 (wǒ bù zhīdào) - I don't know
* 请问 (qǐng wèn) - Excuse me
* 可以吗？(kě yǐ ma?) - Can I?
* 多少钱？(duō shǎo qián?) - How much?

Here are some tips specific to learning basic Mandarin phrases:

* Start by learning the most common phrases, such as greetings, thank you, and goodbye.
* Don't worry about making mistakes at first. Everyone makes mistakes when they are learning a new language.
* Practice speaking and listening to Mandarin as much as possible. You can do this by watching Chinese movies and TV shows, listening to Chinese music, or talking to native Chinese speakers.
* Find a language learning method that works for you. There are many different ways to learn Mandarin, so experiment until you find one that you enjoy and that helps you make progress.

I hope this helps!
