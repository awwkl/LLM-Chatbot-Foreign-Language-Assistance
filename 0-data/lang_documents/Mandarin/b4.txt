Here are some examples of how to thank someone in Mandarin, formatted in point format, with pronunciation:

* 谢谢 (xiè xiè) - Thank you
* 谢谢你 (xiè xiè nǐ) - Thank you (to one person)
* 谢谢你们 (xiè xiè nǐ men) - Thank you (to multiple people)
* 非常感谢 (fēi cháng gǎn xiè) - Thank you very much
* 太感谢你了 (tài gǎn xiè nǐ le) - Thank you so much
* 不客气 (bú kè qi) - You're welcome

Here are some tips specific to thanking someone in Mandarin:

* If you are thanking someone for a gift, you can say 谢谢你的礼物 (xiè xiè nǐ de lǐ wù), which means "thank you for your gift."
* If you are thanking someone for a favor, you can say 谢谢你帮我 (xiè xiè nǐ bāng wǒ), which means "thank you for helping me."
* If you are thanking someone for a compliment, you can say 谢谢你的夸奖 (xiè xiè nǐ de kuā jiǎng), which means "thank you for your compliment."
