How to ask where is the nearest XXX in Italian:

XXX can be replaced with any type of place or thing, such as:

* ristorante (restaurant)
* banca (bank)
* farmacia (pharmacy)
* supermercato (supermarket)
* autobus (bus)
* metro (metro)
* bagno (bathroom)
* ufficio postale (post office)
* ospedale (hospital)
* hotel (hotel)
* museo (museum)
* taxi (taxi)

Example phrases:

* Dov'è il ristorante più vicino? (Where is the nearest restaurant? - Doh-vay eel ree-stoh-rahn-tay pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è la banca più vicina? (Where is the nearest bank? - Doh-vay eel bahn-kah pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è la farmacia più vicina? (Where is the nearest pharmacy? - Doh-vay eel fahr-mah-chee-ah pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è il supermercato più vicino? (Where is the nearest supermarket? - Doh-vay eel soo-per-mer-kah-toh pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è l'autobus più vicino? (Where is the nearest bus? - Doh-vay eel l'ah-oo-toh-boos pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è la metro più vicina? (Where is the nearest metro? - Doh-vay eel lah meh-troh pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è il bagno più vicino? (Where is the nearest bathroom? - Doh-vay eel l'bah-nyoh pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è l'ufficio postale più vicino? (Where is the nearest post office? - Doh-vay eel l'oo-fee-choh poh-stah-lay pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è l'ospedale più vicino? (Where is the nearest hospital? - Doh-vay eel l'oh-spe-dah-lay pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è l'hotel più vicino? (Where is the nearest hotel? - Doh-vay eel l'oh-tel pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è il museo più vicino? (Where is the nearest museum? - Doh-vay eel l'moo-zay-oh pyo vee-chee-noh?)
* Dov'è il taxi più vicino? (Where is the nearest taxi? - Doh-vay eel l'tah-ksee pyo vee-chee-noh?)

Tips:

* Use the correct pronunciation. Italian pronunciation can be tricky, so it is important to practice saying the phrases above correctly. The pronunciation guide above should help, but you can also listen to native Italian speakers saying the phrases online or in Italian movies and TV shows.
* Be polite. When asking for directions in Italian, it is always polite to start with the word "scusi" (excuse me). You can also say "per favore" (please) at the end of your request.
* Be specific. If you are looking for a specific type of place or thing, be sure to mention it in your request. For example, if you are looking for a restaurant that serves traditional Italian food, you could say "Dov'è il ristorante italiano più vicino?" (Where is the nearest Italian restaurant?)
