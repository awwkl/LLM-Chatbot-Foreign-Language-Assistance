* Italian words: Dov'è il Colosseo? (Where is the Colosseum? / DO-VAY eel COL-o-SAY-oh)
* English translation: Where is the Colosseum?
* English pronunciation: DO-VAY eel COL-o-SAY-oh

* English translation: How do I get to the train station?
* English pronunciation: CO-may see VAH ahl-lah sta-ZIO-nay day TRAY-nee

* Italian words: Scusi, sa dov'è il bagno? (Excuse me, do you know where the bathroom is? / SKOO-zee SAH DO-vay eel BAH-nyo)

* If you are asking for general directions, you can omit the definite article.
